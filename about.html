About Us

As an eco-friendly hyper-local marketplace, CornerDukaan has done the work so you don’t have to. Here, you can pick from products that are sourced, manufactured, and shipped in a way that protects our planet’s future. Each brand we’ve partnered with has been chosen because they’re taking the right steps and helping to create a more sustainable future.

At CornerDukaan, we make it simple for you to buy high-quality, reliable products that are produced using sustainable methods and materials. We’re giving you access to sellers, manufacturers, and entrepreneurs who are committed to creating the products you want in a way that respects the Earth. We believe there shouldn’t be a conflict between environmentally sustainable and high quality. The products you find on CornerDukaan are both.

Every product on CornerDukaan is HONESTLY PRICED. They come directly from the manufacturer, so there is no mediator.

Our Story

Our planet is changing.

It’s time for the way we shop to change, too.

The way we shop is easy and convenient. Do you need or want something? Just head to the store or shop online, and it’s yours. But here’s the problem: Too much of the stuff we buy today are made without any thought about tomorrow. Our landfills are growing while our resources are disappearing–it’s just not sustainable. The future of our planet depends on the actions we take today, but the truth is, it’s just not that easy to make every shopping decision a mindful one.

What if there was a better way to shop? The ease and convenience you’re used to, but everything’s made in a way that treats our planet kindly.

That’s where CornerDukaan comes in.

Our goal is to make buying responsibly second nature. We’re on a mission to make it normal to consume in a way that’s easier on the environment, while meeting the the convenience we’ve come to expect.

But this is not a story about CornerDukaan. This is a story about all of us.
