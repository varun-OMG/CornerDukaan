About me

Hailing from a family of farmers, I believe that the key to success is hard work. After my family lost their means of livelihood during the Covid-19 pandemic, I started working to support my family. CornerDukaan has provided me with a healthy and supportive work environment that has enabled me to perform better with confidence and feel motivated about my work. When I’m not working, you can find me singing or reading books on law and government policies.

About Our Venture

CornerDukaan serves fresh, healthy and temptingly tasty snacks, completely made from organic raw materials. These snacks, or rather, mid-day fuels, are stacked with essential nutrients that give a boost of energy. They are light yet fulfilling, giving a sense of completeness and satiating one’s cravings. Our aim is to create a healthier nation by serving people with organic and healthy food and create a business ecosystem that involves and empower women with varied skill sets from different walks of life.

Our USP

Our products are completely organic and the omission of the use of any kind of artificial processing makes it good for your health and also the environment. Our priority is service to humanity and mother earth. Behind the prosperity of our business is the growth of several women and marginalised farmers.
